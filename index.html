Frequently Asked Questions

Who is eligible for AJFI Scholarship?

+

Students from Class 5-12 studying in any recognized board (WBBSE, CBSE, ICSE, State Boards) are eligible. The scholarship considers academic performance, family income, and special categories.

What documents are required?

+

Required documents include: Academic transcripts, Income certificate, Identity proof, Passport size photo, and any relevant certificates for special categories.

What is the scholarship amount?

+

Scholarship amounts vary based on class level and financial need, ranging from ‚Çπ5,000 to ‚Çπ50,000 per year. The exact amount is determined after application review.

How long does the application process take?

+

The complete application process takes 4-6 weeks, including document verification, mentor review, and committee evaluation.

Can I reapply if not selected?

+

Yes, you can reapply in the next academic year. We encourage students to improve their academic performance and strengthen their application.

Is there any application fee?

+

No, the AJFI Scholarship application is completely free. We believe in providing equal opportunities to all students.
